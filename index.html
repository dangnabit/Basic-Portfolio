About Me

Dan Gabel is a Lighting Designer and aspiring Full Stack Developer living in Chicago. Mulder, his chuggle (chihuahua, beagle, pug mix) wakes him up every morning to start his days.


On a typical day, Dan can be found doing anything from installing large sound and lighting systems in nightclubs to writing plugins for GrandMA2.


His favorite activities include replaying NES classics and arguing with his friends about who is the top heel on Monday Night Raw.
